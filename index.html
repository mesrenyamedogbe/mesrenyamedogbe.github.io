Open Source Advocate & Community Builder

Hello! I’m Mesrenyame Dogbe, a passionate advocate for open source and inclusivity in tech. I host Everything Open Source Twitter Spaces where I invite professionals to chat about different Open Source topics, and I love building and supporting communities around technology and collaboration.

I’m always looking to collaborate with Open Source communities and projects on community and programmes management. Ask me about tech community building, research in open source or simply call me Golden – that’s my fun fact!

About Me

Based in Accra, Ghana and active in global tech circles, I work at the intersection of community, research and open source. I believe that collaboration and knowledge sharing are the cornerstones of innovation. Over the years I’ve volunteered and led programmes that uplift underrepresented groups in technology, organised international conferences and contributed to numerous open source projects. My experience spans programme management, mentorship and advocacy, culminating in recognition across the industry.
